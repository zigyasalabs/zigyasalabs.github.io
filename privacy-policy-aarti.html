Privacy Policy — Aarti Collection (आरती संग्रह)

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Overview

Aarti Collection is a devotional audio app for listening to Hindu aartis, following lyrics, and tracking recitation counts. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information. The app does not require an account, and nothing you do in the app is sent to our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email, location, audio input, usage analytics, or any data from your contacts, camera, or storage.

Data Stored on Your Device

The only data saved is your selected loop count (1, 3, 5, 11, or ∞), stored locally in Android SharedPreferences. It is never transmitted anywhere.

App Permissions

  • INTERNET — to load ads and fetch Firebase remote config
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to check connectivity before loading ads
  • AD_ID — required by Google Mobile Ads SDK
  • WAKE_LOCK — to keep the screen on during audio playback

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob — serves banner ads and may collect device identifiers and general location (from IP) to show relevant ads. You can opt out via Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase Remote Config — used to toggle app features remotely. Collects anonymous instance data only. Privacy info: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Children's Privacy

Aarti Collection is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a revised date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance.
