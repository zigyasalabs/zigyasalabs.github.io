Privacy Policy
Hindi Kahaniyaan · com.zigyasalabs.hindikahaniyaan Last updated: June 29, 2025
Hindi Kahaniyaan is a free, offline Hindi stories app. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains clearly what information is collected, why, and how it is handled. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information with third parties beyond what is described below.
01
Information We Collect

The app stores the following data locally on your device only. None of this information is transmitted to our servers.

Data Purpose Stored
Saved (favourite) stories So you can revisit bookmarked stories Device only
Last read story IDs To show "Continue Reading" on the home screen Device only
Font size preference To remember your reading comfort setting Device only
Theme preference (light/dark/system) To remember your display setting Device only
Personal identity or account data — Not collected

All story content (1,100+ stories) is bundled within the app itself. No internet connection is required to read stories.

02
Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services which may collect limited, non-personal information as described:

Google AdMob
Used to display banner advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalisation in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Ads can be disabled by us via remote configuration at any time.

Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
Firebase Remote Config
Used to remotely toggle features (such as enabling or disabling ads) without requiring an app update. No personal data is sent through this service. Firebase may collect basic device and app metadata.

Firebase Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
03
App Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Required to load advertisements and fetch remote configuration.

No camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage permissions are requested.

04
Children's Privacy

Hindi Kahaniyaan is a general-audience app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not include registration, chat, or social features of any kind.

05
Data Security

All user preferences are stored using Android's standard SharedPreferences mechanism and are accessible only by this app on your device. We do not transmit this data anywhere. Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no risk of a server-side data breach affecting you.

06
Story Sharing

When you use the Share button on a story, the app passes the story title, a short excerpt, and a link to the Play Store listing to your device's native share sheet. This action is entirely under your control — no data is sent to our servers, and we have no visibility into where you share.

07
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
