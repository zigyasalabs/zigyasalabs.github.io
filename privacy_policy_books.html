Privacy Policy
English Novel & Books Offline

Effective: June 28, 2026 Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the English Novel & Books Offline Android app ("the App") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We are committed to keeping your data minimal and your reading experience private.

Information We Collect

The App is designed to work offline. Most of your data never leaves your device. A small amount of anonymous information is collected automatically to help us keep the App stable and improve it over time.

What Why & how
App usage & screen events Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage events (e.g. which category you open, page reads). No personally identifiable information is attached.
Crash & error reports Firebase Crashlytics captures crash logs, device model, OS version, and app version when the App crashes. This data is used solely for debugging.
Advertising identifiers Google AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID to serve and measure ads. You can reset or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings.
Favourite books list Stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.
Reading preferences Light/dark theme preference is stored locally on your device only.
No account required. The App does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information. No user registration exists.

How We Use the Information

  • To understand which features and books are most used, so we can improve the App.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs quickly.
  • To display ads that help us keep the App free for readers.
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent any information to third parties.
  • We do not use any information for user profiling or targeted marketing of our own.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party SDKs. Each has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data.

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) — anonymous event tracking. Privacy policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) — crash reporting. Privacy policy
  • Google AdMob — in-app advertising. Privacy policy
  • Firebase Remote Config (Google) — used to manage app settings remotely; no personal data is sent. Privacy policy

These services operate under their own terms. We encourage you to review their policies if you have questions about how they handle data.

Internet Permission

The App requests internet access solely for: loading ads (when ads are enabled), sending crash reports to Crashlytics, and fetching remote configuration values. Book content is bundled with the App and is fully available offline — no content is downloaded from the internet.

Data Retention & Security

Data stored on your device (favourites, preferences) is retained until you uninstall the App. Anonymous analytics and crash data is retained by Google in accordance with their data retention policies.

We do not operate our own servers and therefore do not store any personal information on external infrastructure controlled by Zigyasalabs.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Ads served by AdMob are configured to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) where applicable.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Your Choices

  • Ad personalisation: Go to Settings → Google → Ads on your Android device to opt out of personalised ads or reset your advertising ID.
  • Analytics opt-out: Firebase Analytics respects the Android advertising opt-out setting. Disabling ad personalisation also limits Analytics data collection.
  • Favourites & preferences: Clear app data via Settings → Apps → English Novel & Books Offline → Clear Data to remove all locally stored data.
  • Uninstall: Removing the App deletes all locally stored data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

For significant changes we will make reasonable efforts to notify users via an in-app notice.
