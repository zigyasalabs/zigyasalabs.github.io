Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2025

Zigyasa Labs built the Paheliyaan – हिंदी पहेलियाँ app as a free app. This page informs users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Paheliyaan does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. We do not store any user-generated content on our servers.

2. Information Collected Automatically

To improve app performance and serve relevant advertisements, certain data is collected automatically by third-party services integrated into the app:

2a. Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data and interaction with ads
  • General location (country/region, derived from IP)

This data is used to show personalised or non-personalised ads. Google's privacy policy governs AdMob data practices: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2b. Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app (e.g., which screens are viewed most). Firebase Analytics collects:

  • App usage events (screen views, button taps)
  • Device type, OS version, and language
  • Anonymised demographic information

No personally identifiable information is linked to these analytics events. Firebase Analytics privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

2c. Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix app crashes. When a crash occurs, Crashlytics collects:

  • Crash stack traces and diagnostics
  • Device model, OS version
  • App version and state at the time of crash

This data is used solely for debugging and improving app stability. No personal data is collected. Crashlytics privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3. How We Use Information

The data collected through third-party SDKs is used to:

  • Display advertisements to support the free app
  • Understand which features are used most to improve the app
  • Identify and fix crashes and bugs

We do not sell, trade, or rent any user data to third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

Paheliyaan is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. The third-party SDKs (AdMob, Firebase) are configured to comply with applicable children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our own servers. Data collected by Google AdMob and Firebase is retained according to Google's own data retention policies.

6. Security

We rely on commercially reasonable means to protect information processed through third-party services. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to the Google Play Store or other external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting to this page. We recommend reviewing this page periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
